Preface

...I'll let you be the judge, of your endeavors.

"Bhaiya 2 packet milk & a kings' pack."

It's a simple but classy way of beginning a day. A sweet, refreshing, yet nostalgic taste of homemade tea, brings back the pace of normal life, leisurely. And for those who enjoy smoking, it brings a sense of content, yet longing for more.

Life is an exceptional journey, however mundane it becomes. While most find comfort in the warmth of known, some take upon themselves to wander through unknown, seeking, searching, in hope to break out from the Vicious Cycle. A journey that might one day conclude or maybe left just a few steps short. Whatever the case, is bound to be interesting. At times a quick sip from hot cup of tea, enjoyed with few drags from cigarette, tend to provide right moments to sit down and dream.

Milkandcigarettes.so is a collection of fragments, from the journey of team 27AE60.
